All RIFE2 Examples

All the examples are included in this site. These are their URLs:

HelloAuthentication

  • Open your browser at {{v route:/}}authentication
  • The authentication credentials are: testUser / testPassword

HelloContinuations

  • Open your browser at {{v route:/}}guess

HelloCounterContinuations

  • Open your browser at {{v route:/}}count

HelloDatabase

  • Install the database at {{v route:/}}install
  • Then add names by going to {{v route:/}}add
  • When you're done, delete the database through {{v route:/}}remove

HelloErrors

  • Trigger and exception at {{v route:/}}error
  • Try any other URL that doesn't exit, ie. {{v route:/}}treasure

HelloForm

  • Open your browser at {{v route:/}}form

HelloFormContinuations

  • Open your browser at {{v route:/}}continuation/form

HelloGenericQueryManager

  • Install the database at {{v route:/}}generic/install
  • Then add names by going to {{v route:/}}generic/add
  • Delete the database through {{v route:/}}generic/remove

HelloGroup

  • Open your browser at {{v route:/}}welcome
  • Also at {{v route:/}}group/hello
  • And at {{v route:/}}group/bonjour

HelloLink

  • Open your browser at {{v route:/}}link

HelloPathInfoMapping

  • Open your browser at {{v route:/}}mapping/John
  • Or {{v route:/}}mapping/John/Smith
  • Or another other matching path info

HelloSvg

  • Open your browser at {{v route:/}}clock

HelloTemplate

  • Open your browser at {{v route:/}}template

HelloValidation

  • Install the database at {{v route:/}}validation/install
  • Then add names by going to {{v route:/}}validation/add
  • Delete the database through {{v route:/}}validation/remove

HelloWorld

  • Open your browser at {{v route:/}}hello
