All RIFE2 Examples

All the examples are included in this site. These are their URLs:

HelloAuthentication

  • Open your browser at http://localhost:8080/authentication
  • The authentication credentials are: testUser / testPassword

HelloContinuations

  • Open your browser at http://localhost:8080/guess

HelloCounterContinuations

  • Open your browser at http://localhost:8080/count

HelloDatabase

  • Install the database at http://localhost:8080/install
  • Then add names by going to http://localhost:8080/add
  • When you're done, delete the database through http://localhost:8080/remove

HelloErrors

  • Trigger and exception at http://localhost:8080/error
  • Try any other URL that doesn't exit, ie. http://localhost:8080/treasure

HelloForm

  • Open your browser at http://localhost:8080/form

HelloFormContinuations

  • Open your browser at http://localhost:8080/continuation/form

HelloGenericQueryManager

  • Install the database at http://localhost:8080/generic/install
  • Then add names by going to http://localhost:8080/generic/add
  • Delete the database through http://localhost:8080/generic/remove

HelloGroup

  • Open your browser at http://localhost:8080/welcome
  • Also at http://localhost:8080/group/hello
  • And at http://localhost:8080/group/bonjour

HelloLink

  • Open your browser at http://localhost:8080/link

HelloPathInfoMapping

  • Open your browser at http://localhost:8080/mapping/John
  • Or http://localhost:8080/mapping/John/Smith
  • Or another other matching path info

HelloSvg

  • Open your browser at http://localhost:8080/clock

HelloTemplate

  • Open your browser at http://localhost:8080/template

HelloValidation

  • Install the database at http://localhost:8080/validation/install
  • Then add names by going to http://localhost:8080/validation/add
  • Delete the database through http://localhost:8080/validation/remove

HelloWorld

  • Open your browser at http://localhost:8080/hello
